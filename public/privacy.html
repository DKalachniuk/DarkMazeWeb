Privacy Policy for Dark Maze

Last Updated: March 19, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Dark Dark Maze. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you play our game.

Information We Collect

Game Center Data

  • Your Game Center player ID
  • Game progress and achievements
  • Leaderboard scores
  • Level completion times

Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Game performance data
  • Crash reports

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Track your game progress
  • Display your scores on leaderboards
  • Award achievements
  • Improve game performance
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

Data Storage

  • Game progress is stored locally on your device
  • Leaderboard scores and achievements are stored on Apple's Game Center servers
  • We do not store any personal information on our servers

Third-Party Services

Game Center

Our game uses Apple's Game Center service to:

  • Track and display leaderboard scores
  • Manage achievements
  • Enable social features

Game Center's use of your information is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your Game Center data through your device settings
  • Reset your game progress
  • Opt out of Game Center features
  • Delete your game data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: dkalachniuk@gmail.com

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Apple's App Store Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
